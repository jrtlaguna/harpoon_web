Our Story

Harpoon Solution was created by Nathalie Rodrigues, Brian Erasmus, Sam Hatchwell, Rebecca Smith and Bintu Omagbemi, after more than a decade of combined professional yacht experience. We saw a clear opportunity to bring digital connectivity to the yachting world, and resolve service gaps in capabilities, safety, and overall success and satisfaction

Equipped with the knowledge developed over years on the open water, we understand the ins-and-outs of planning provisions, gathering preferences, and budgeting. From that expertise, we were able to develop a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that allows guests and crew to simultaneously and efficiently access information that is important for creating a bespoke luxury vacation.

We believe that keeping centralized information and seamless communication guides our ongoing development of Harpoon Solution. The result is a product that helps provide first-class experiences with minimal disruptions.
